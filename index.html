About the author

Veteran of the mic, student for life

Jake Abdullah is a veteran of the Malaysian media and entertainment scene, with a career spanning over three decades. He started as a music director and announcer at Hitz FM in 1996, rose through the ranks, and was named CEO of Astro Radio in 2013. Off-air, he's still spinning records occasionally as DJ Jakeman, hosting a weekly show on Fly FM.

He holds an MBA from the University of Liverpool, has completed the Harvard Senior Managers Program, and is currently pursuing a PhD at Universiti Utara Malaysia. He also serves as an adjunct professor at UNITAR University and Taylor's College, and sits on the Industry Advisory Board at UCSI University.
